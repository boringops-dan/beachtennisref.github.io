HistoryWhere Beach Tennis Came From

From an Italian beach to a global ITF sport. the short history of beach tennis.

ItalyBrazilITF

Beach tennis originated on the beaches of Italy (around Ravenna) in the 1970s-1980s as a casual paddle game, was formalized into a competitive sport, and was adopted by the International Tennis Federation (ITF) in 2008. It is now most popular in Italy, Brazil, and Spain, with a growing global tour.

The Story

From Beach Game to ITF Sport.

What began as Italians knocking a ball back and forth with wooden paddles on the Adriatic coast grew into an organized sport with rules, rankings, and equipment standards. Brazil embraced it enthusiastically, becoming a powerhouse, and the game spread across Europe and the Americas.

The ITF brought beach tennis under its umbrella in 2008, standardizing the rules, court dimensions, and equipment, and launching a world tour. Today it is one of the fastest-growing beach sports.
