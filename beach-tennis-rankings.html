Beach tennis rankings are run by the International Tennis Federation through the ITF Beach Tennis Tour. players earn ranking points based on how far they advance at sanctioned events, which are tiered by prize money and importance. Points accumulate into a world ranking, and the season builds toward the ITF World Championships.

The Detail

How the Rankings Work.

The ITF Beach Tennis Tour runs sanctioned tournaments worldwide, tiered by category. higher-tier events award more ranking points and bigger prize money. Players collect points by reaching later rounds, and those points form the world ranking that seeds future events.

The calendar builds toward the ITF Beach Tennis World Championships and team competitions. Italy and Brazil dominate the top of the rankings, reflecting where the sport is most established. For most players, the ranking matters mainly for seeding at the events they enter.
