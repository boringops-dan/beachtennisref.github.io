Put a live, auto-updating score on your beach tennis stream.

To add a beach tennis scoreboard overlay to your stream, score the match in BeachTennisRef.App, open its live shareable score link, and add that link as a Browser Source in OBS, vMix, or Streamlabs. The on-screen score then updates live as you score, with no manual typing during the match.

How To

Getting the Score On Screen.

  1. Score in the app. run the match in BeachTennisRef.App so the score is live and accurate to ITF rules.
  2. Open the live share link. the app gives you a shareable live-score page that updates in real time.
  3. Add a Browser Source. in OBS / vMix / Streamlabs, add a Browser Source and paste that link as the URL.
  4. Size and place it. position the score where you want it on your scene, like any other overlay layer.
  5. Score as normal. every point you record updates the overlay automatically. no typing into the streaming software.

This is the same approach the big multi-sport scoreboard tools use, applied to beach tennis with correct scoring built in. a live beach tennis scoreboard that doubles as your broadcast graphic. For multi-court events, see the tournament scoreboard.

In the App

Score It With BeachTennisRef.App.

  • BeachTennisRef.App produces a live, shareable score link you can drop straight into your streaming software as a browser source.
  • The score follows real beach tennis rules. no-ad golden point, the tie-break, ends changes. so your broadcast graphic is always correct.
  • Works for a single court or a whole tournament.
