More Than a Score Counter.

A plain scoreboard just shows two numbers. A beach tennis scoreboard has to know whose serve it is, when the tie-break rotation flips (ITF Rule 8b), and when to change ends. BeachTennisRef.App is a free online scoreboard that does all of that automatically. you tap who won the point and it keeps the rest straight.

Open it in any browser. phone, tablet, or laptop. no app store, no install. Add it to your home screen for one tap access courtside.
