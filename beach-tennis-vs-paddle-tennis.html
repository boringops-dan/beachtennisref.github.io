Beach Tennisvs Paddle Sports.

Beach tennis, paddle tennis, platform tennis, pop tennis, padel. constantly confused. Here is the difference.

DisambiguationCourtPlay style
Try BeachTennisRef FreeRead the answer

Beach tennis is a sand-court, no-bounce volley game with a 1.70 m net. It is distinct from paddle tennis and pop tennis (hard-court, lower net, ball bounces), platform tennis (a small heated court with screens, played in cold weather), and padel (an enclosed court with glass walls). The shared thread is a solid paddle, but the surfaces and rules are entirely different.
