WhereBeach Tennis Indoors

No beach, no problem. the game moves inside in winter. Here is how.

Indoor sandYear-roundSame rules
Try BeachTennisRef FreeRead more

Yes, you can play beach tennis indoors. dedicated indoor sand arenas (often shared with beach volleyball) host it year-round, which is how the sport stays active through winter in colder countries. The rules, court, and net height are identical to outdoor play; you simply lose the wind and sun.

The Detail

Indoor Beach Tennis.

Indoor beach-sport centres lay deep sand courts under a roof, so the surface and dimensions match an outdoor court exactly. same 16 m x 8 m court, same 1.70 m net.

The main differences are environmental: no wind to read on lobs and serves, consistent lighting, and stable temperature. That makes indoor play great for learning and league seasons. In much of Europe, indoor arenas are why beach tennis is a year-round sport, not just a summer one. See where to play.
