# Flat Serve - Beach Tennis Glossary

> A flat serve is a service delivery hit with minimal spin, producing a fast, direct ball flight through the service box. The flat serve travels at maximum speed but with less margin for error compared to a spin serve. The ball must land in the correct service box (the diagonal box on the server's right or left depending on service order). Per ITF Rules, if a flat serve lands outside the service box it is a service fault.

**Ruleset:** ITF
**Rule Reference:** ITF Beach Tennis Rules, Rule 7 (The Service)

**Related Terms:** spin-serve, service-fault, ace
