# Mixed Doubles - Beach Tennis Glossary

> Mixed doubles is the format where each team consists of one male and one female player. Mixed doubles follows the same ITF Beach Tennis Rules as standard doubles, including the serve order (one player serves a full game, alternating with their partner each game), the receiving order (partners alternate receiving serve), and the tie-break service rotation. Mixed doubles is one of the primary competitive formats at ITF and national beach tennis events.

**Ruleset:** ITF
**Rule Reference:** ITF Beach Tennis Rules, Rule 8 (Order of Service in Doubles)

**Related Terms:** doubles-formation, service-order-doubles
