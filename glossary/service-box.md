# Service Box - Beach Tennis Glossary

> The service box is the rectangular area in the opponent's court into which the serve must land to be good. In beach tennis, the service box is the full opposite half of the court divided diagonally: the server serves from the right half of the baseline to the diagonal left service box, and from the left half to the diagonal right service box. Per ITF Beach Tennis Rules Rule 7, the ball must land in the correct service box; landing outside or on a line belonging to the wrong box is a service fault. The service box lines are good (the ball is in).

**Ruleset:** ITF
**Rule Reference:** ITF Beach Tennis Rules, Rule 7 (The Service)

**Related Terms:** service-fault, baseline, sideline
