# Warm-Up - Beach Tennis Glossary

> The warm-up is the pre-match practice period during which both players or pairs hit on the court to prepare physically before official play begins. Per ITF Beach Tennis Rules, a standard warm-up of 5 minutes is allowed before official play; this time is for hitting practice only and not for practicing serves competitively. Neither player may claim points during the warm-up. The warm-up period ends when the first server is determined and the match formally begins.

**Ruleset:** ITF
**Rule Reference:** ITF Beach Tennis Rules (Pre-Match Procedures)

**Related Terms:** match, service-order-doubles
