The Problem

Beach Tennis Scoring Is Fiddly.

The serve rotation, the tie-break, the ends changes. easy to lose track between rallies in the sun.

"Whose serve is it?"

Serve order changes by set, and the tie-break has its own rotation. Lose the thread for one point and the whole game is in dispute.

Tie-Break & Ends Changes

No-advantage scoring, the match tie-break, changing ends at the right interval. Generic tennis apps get the beach tennis nuances wrong.

Counting in Your Head

You came to play, not to be a human scoreboard. And there's nowhere to share the score with whoever's watching from the sand.

Features

The Rules Are Handled. Just Play.

Serve rotation, tie-breaks, ends changes, lets. enforced automatically. You stop counting and start playing.

ITF Rules Enforced Automatically

Serve rotation per set, no-advantage scoring, the match tie-break, ends changes. It is a beach tennis app, not a tennis app with a beach mode. The rules are built in.

Tie-Break Serving (Rule 8b)

The tie-break serve alternates on its own schedule. The app tracks exactly who serves each point so you never have to argue it mid-breaker.

Court & Serve Tracking

Always know who serves, which side each pair is on, and when ends change. The court view shows the serving player and prompts the change of ends.

Full Undo

Wrong tap on a point, a let, the first server? Undo instantly. Every action is reversible. No restarting the game, no panic.

Works Offline

Score a full match with no signal or WiFi. The app saves everything locally and syncs when you reconnect. Made for the beach, not just the clubhouse.

Live Scoreboard Sharing

Share a live link. Friends on the sand and fans at home follow every point in real time. current serve, set scores, match status. No app install for them.

Singles & Doubles

Correct serve order and rules for both formats. Player names show on the scoreboard, so spectators always know who they are watching.

Built for the Court

Big buttons, clear layout, one-handed scoring in bright sun. Fast and clean, with no hunting through menus between points.

Match History

Every match is saved automatically. Review past matches, share results, and keep a record of the whole tournament or season.

How It Works

Set Up. Serve. Score.

Three steps from first serve to final point.

1

Set Up Your Match

Enter the players or pairs, pick singles or doubles, and choose the format. ITF defaults are ready to go, so you are scoring in under a minute.

2

Pick the First Server

Tap who serves first. From there the app handles serve rotation, the tie-break order, and ends changes automatically.

3

Score the Match

Tap to score each point. Serve, sets, tie-breaks, and ends changes are tracked for you. Share a live link, and review the full match afterward.
