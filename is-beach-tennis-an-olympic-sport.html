Beach tennis is not currently an Olympic sport. It is governed by the International Tennis Federation (ITF), which runs a global Beach Tennis Tour and an annual World Championships with world rankings. the structure of an established sport, but it has not been added to the Olympic programme.

The Detail

Where the Sport Stands.

Since the ITF adopted beach tennis in 2008, it has built the framework of a serious international sport: standardized rules, a world tour, ranking points, and a World Championships. It has also featured in multi-sport events like the World Beach Games.

But Olympic inclusion is a separate, lengthy process, and beach tennis is not on the Olympic programme. Its fast global growth makes it a candidate for the future, but for now it remains an ITF-governed world-tour sport, not an Olympic one.
