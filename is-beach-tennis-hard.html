Getting StartedIs Beach Tennis Hard?

The honest answer for anyone thinking about trying it.

Beginner-friendlyEasy startNo bounce
Try BeachTennisRef FreeRead more

No. beach tennis is easy to pick up, especially if you have played any racket sport or beach volleyball. Rallies are short, the court is small, and the depressurized ball is forgiving. mastering the smash and serve placement is what takes practice.

The Detail

Easy to Start, Deep to Master.

What is easy: the basics. The small court, slow ball, and no-bounce volley game mean beginners are rallying within minutes. Doubles also shares the load with a partner.

What takes practice: the overhead smash, serve placement (you usually get one serve), and net positioning in doubles. See beginner tips and how to play.
