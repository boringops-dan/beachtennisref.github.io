Description of Service

BeachTennisRef.App is a beach tennis scorekeeping application available as a web app and as a native mobile app on iOS and Android. It allows users to score beach tennis matches with automatic rule enforcement for FIVB, USAV, NFHS, and NCAA rulesets. Features include substitution tracking, libero management, rotation enforcement, sanction recording, live scoreboard sharing, and PDF scoresheet export.

The web app is accessible through modern browsers on any device. The mobile apps are available through the Apple App Store and Google Play Store.
