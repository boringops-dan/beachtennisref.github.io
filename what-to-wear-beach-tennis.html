Getting StartedWhat to Wear for Beach Tennis

You need less than you think. Here is the simple kit list for playing on sand.

Bare feetSun protectionLight kit
Try BeachTennisRef FreeRead more

For beach tennis, wear light, breathable athletic clothing (a tank or tee and shorts), strong sun protection (hat, sunglasses, sunscreen), and play barefoot or in sand socks for grip and to keep the heat off. No special shoes are needed since the game is played on sand.

The Detail

Gearing Up for the Sand.

  • Clothing. light, breathable, moisture-wicking. you will sweat in the sun and sand.
  • Feet. barefoot is standard; sand socks add grip and protect from hot sand.
  • Sun protection. hat or visor, sunglasses, and water-resistant sunscreen. reapply often.
  • Hydration. bring more water than you think; playing on sand is demanding.
  • Gear. just a paddle and balls. see the full equipment guide.
