Get StartedWhere to Play Beach Tennis

No court near you? There might be more than you think. Here is how to find a place to play.

ClubsBeachesLeagues
Try BeachTennisRef FreeRead more

To find beach tennis near you, check local beach-sport and tennis clubs, search your national federation’s club finder, look at beach volleyball venues (they often add beach tennis nets), and search social platforms for local groups. If nothing exists, you can set up your own court with minimal gear.

How To

Finding a Place to Play.

  • Federation club finders: many national tennis federations now list beach tennis clubs and events.
  • Beach volleyball venues: they share the same court footprint and increasingly add beach tennis nets. just ask.
  • Social & maps: search "beach tennis" plus your city on Instagram, Facebook groups, and map apps.
  • Indoor sand centres: in colder climates, indoor beach-sport arenas often run beach tennis.
  • Start your own: with a net and paddles you can set up a court on any suitable beach.
